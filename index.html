PhD Student, Columbia Business School
Decision, Risk, and Operations

I am a PhD student at Columbia Business School in the Decision, Risk, and Operations division. I am advised by Jing Dong and Hongseok Namkoong.

My research is on designing scalable and structured policy optimization approaches for operational and decision problems, using tools from reinforcement learning, approximate dynamic programming, and optimal control. In particular, I am interested in applications in adaptive experimentation and routing control in queuing systems. I've also done research in mechanism design.
